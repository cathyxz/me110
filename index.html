ME 110

Product Visualization

Overview

In the first 5 weeks of this class, we cover all the basic tools necessary for visualizing products with your pen:

  • Construction and composition of basic shapes
  • Perspective
  • Orthographic drawing
  • Shading in 3D
  • Rendering surface textures

We spend the second half of the class perfecting these techniques on two major projects: rendering a film scene, and a product sketch.
